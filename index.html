That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I had found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work, and also a way to give my music a voice. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organizational skills and eye for detail, as well as my love of patterns to write clean, effective code.
